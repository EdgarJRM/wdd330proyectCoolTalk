Welcome to Bountiful Foods

Welcome to Bountiful Foods Bountiful Foods is an American agribusiness company founded in 2006 and based in Carlsbad, California. We manufacture and export high-quality products. The company sells fruits and products directly from farmers in the area but also uses some national and international suppliers. We provide our high-quality products to many food industry companies worldwide.

Mission

Cooperate in the cultural and economic development of communities and associations. Offer the highest quality products to our customers and give the best of ourselves to the world to achieve satisfaction and preference.

View

To be recognized worldwide as the export company that promotes fair trade with products of the highest quality.

Socioeconomic Development

Bountiful Foods has been promoting the development of productive chains of organic products with producers, the direct purchase of their crops and providing them with specialized technical assistance.
